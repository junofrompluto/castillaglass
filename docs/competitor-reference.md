# Competitor & Positioning Reference

Working notes for the Castilla Glass site. Not published — internal reference.

## Where Castilla sits

**Castilla Glass is the installation company for V&V Windows & Doors** (the parent
manufacturer). That's the core positioning advantage: **factory-direct impact
windows & doors, installed by the same family.**

- **V&V Windows & Doors** — https://vvwindowsimpact.com/ · (305) 888-4151
  - Family-owned **impact window/door manufacturer**, 25+ years, Opa-Locka FL.
  - Sells **direct from manufacturer** ("no middle man"). Series 100–450 residential;
    commercial storefronts / window walls; thermal-break energy systems; pergolas.
  - **Miami-Dade NOA** approved, NFRC rated, hurricane / wind-borne-debris rated.
  - Blocks up to 99% UV, laminated glass w/ PVB interlayer, 100% financing available.
  - Blues/whites brand, safety-focused tone.
- **Castilla Glass** — the install + glass-service arm: impact window/door install,
  shower doors, glass railings, storefronts, repair & 24/7 emergency.

> Takeaway: lead with the manufacturer tie. "You're buying factory-direct and having
> it installed by the manufacturer's own crew — Miami-Dade NOA product, one accountable
> family, no subcontractors."

## Competitor 1 — Structure Glass Solutions (structuregs.com)

Glass **hardware supplier**, Hialeah Gardens FL (local). B2B, product-catalog focus.

- Shower systems (Bayline, Flux, Element, Quatro, Streamline, Parallel), hardware,
  architectural railings/closers, glazing supplies, glass machinery, spare parts.
- 10+ yrs, physical address, warranty/refund/privacy pages, newsletter, SMS opt-in.
- **Notable:** a **glass weight calculator** tool, "Request a Quote"/"Quick Order",
  catalog download. Blue/grey, straightforward.
- **Adopt:** the weight-calculator tool (done), quote CTA prominence.

## Competitor 2 — ASP SuperHome (aspsuperhome.com)

South Florida **contractor** (impact windows, roofing, solar, remodeling). Master
class in trust-building — this is the model to beat for the marketing site.

- Tagline: "Transform Your Home Into a SuperHome: Stronger, Safer, More Efficient."
- **Trust stack:** 19+ yrs (since 2006), 10,000+ families, 1,900+ 5-star reviews,
  license #s (CCC/CVC/CGC), $2M+ insured, 15+ award badges, **"No subcontractors. Ever."**
- Sections: hero + phone CTA, trust badges, stat carousel, award showcase, service
  cards, gallery, testimonials, "Why choose us" matrix, **$0-down financing**,
  showroom locations, service-area-by-city lists, blog, **FAQ accordion**, footer.
- **Persistent "Get A Free Estimate"** button; phone in every CTA. Blue/white/orange.
- **Adopt:** trust/credibility bar (done), testimonials + FAQ (done), $0-down
  financing (done), service-area-by-city (already have area chips), persistent quote CTA.
